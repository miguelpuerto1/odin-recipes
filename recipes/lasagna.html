lasagna

Description

Image result for lasagna recipe description Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Start by spreading a layer of your tomato-based sauce
  2. Next, add a single layer of pasta sheets
  3. Then, add a layer of ricotta cheese
